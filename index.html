YOUNG KRONES

ENTER THE KRONEDOM

YOUNG KRONES is a theatermaking duo based in Rogers Park, Chicago.

YOUNG KRONES is Ava Calabrese Grob (A KRONE) and Maggie K. Rothberg (M KRONE).

CURRENTLY, KRONES are looking into parties, architectural change, being young and old, moving the audience, gender insubordination, power, and SENSOLOGY.
